Beginner's Guide to the World of Indie Horror Games

Indie horror games have become increasingly popular over the last decade, offering unique and immersive experiences that often differ from mainstream horror titles. These games are typically developed by small teams or even solo developers, allowing for more creative freedom and innovation in the title. Some of the most popular indie horror games include Amnesia: The Dark Descent, Phasmophobia, and Outlast.

If you're interested in exploring more indie horror games, you can browse Steam's Horror Games Tagged by Indie. However, as Steam is user curated, this results in a wide variety of games that may not fit the traditional definition of "horror". That is why I have compiled a list of what I consider to be standout indie horror games that I believe truly capture the essence of the genre. These games are not only terrifying but also offer unique gameplay mechanics and storytelling that set them apart from mainstream horror titles.
