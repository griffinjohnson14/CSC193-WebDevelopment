Beginner's Guide to the World of Indie Horror Games

Indie horror games have become increasingly popular over the last decade, offering unique and immersive experiences that often differ from mainstream horror titles. These games are typically developed by small teams or even solo developers, allowing for more creative freedom and innovation in the title.
